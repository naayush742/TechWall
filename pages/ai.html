The Era of Artificial Intelligence

This page explores the rapid evolution of AI, from foundational algorithms to the multimodal Large Language Models (LLMs) that are redefining human-computer interaction.

Google Gemini

Announced in December 2023, Gemini is Google's most capable and general AI model family. Built by Google DeepMind, it was designed from the ground up to be multimodal, meaning it can understand and operate across text, code, audio, image, and video.

The Gemini family includes models optimized for different tasks: Gemini Ultra for complex reasoning, Gemini Pro for scaling across a wide range of tasks, and Gemini Nano for on-device efficiency. With its long-context window (up to 1M+ tokens), it represents a significant leap in AI capabilities.

Google AI Ecosystem

Google has been a pioneer in AI for decades, from the development of the Transformer architecture in 2017 to the integration of AI across all its products. Through Vertex AI on Google Cloud, they provide a unified platform for training and deploying machine learning models.

Google's commitment to AI is evident in its TPUs (Tensor Processing Units) specifically designed for ML workloads and its research in Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and responsible AI practices that power everything from Search to Waymo's self-driving cars.
